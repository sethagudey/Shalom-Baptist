Soul Winning Strategies

Practical guidance for effective evangelism.

Before Evangelism

• Pray before going out.
• Study God's Word.
• Prepare your testimony.
• Go with compassion and love.
• Trust the Holy Spirit for guidance.


Conversation Starters

• Can I share a message of hope with you?
• Do you attend a church family?
• Can I pray for you today?
• Have you heard about God's love for you?
• What gives you hope in difficult times?


Leading Someone to Christ

1. Share God's Love.
2. Explain Sin and Separation.
3. Present Jesus Christ.
4. Call for Repentance.
5. Lead in Prayer.
6. Encourage Church Fellowship.


Key Scriptures

John 3:16
Romans 3:23
Romans 6:23
Acts 1:8
Matthew 28:19-20


Follow-Up Plan

Day 1 – Phone Call
Day 3 – Prayer Check-In
Day 7 – Invite to Church
Week 2 – Bible Study
Month 1 – Connect to Ministry


Remember

Evangelism is about people, not numbers. Love people, listen carefully, and trust God for the results.
